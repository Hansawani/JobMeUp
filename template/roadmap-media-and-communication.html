Career Roadmap: Media and Communication

  • High School (10+2) Focus on subjects like English, Social Sciences, and Humanities.
  • Entrance Exams Prepare for and take entrance exams for admission into undergraduate media and communication courses in recognized universities.
  • Undergraduate Studies Pursue a Bachelor's degree in Mass Media, Journalism, or Communication Studies.
  • Internships Participate in internships during your course to gain practical experience and understand the workings of the media industry.
  • Postgraduate Studies After your Bachelor's, you can opt for a Master's degree in your chosen specialty within media and communication.
  • Work Experience Start working in the media industry. This could be in journalism, advertising, public relations, digital media, etc.
  • Continuous Learning and Networking Keep yourself updated with the latest trends in the media industry. Attend workshops, seminars, and networking events.
