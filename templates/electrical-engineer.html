Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering (B.E./B.Tech)

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering covers subjects like circuit analysis, electromagnetism, power systems, and electronic devices.

Master’s Degree in Electrical Engineering (M.E./M.Tech)

Duration: 1-2 years

A Master’s degree provides advanced training and specialization in fields such as power systems, control systems, or telecommunications.

Professional Engineer (PE) License

Duration: Varies by region

Obtaining a PE license is essential for higher-level positions and involves passing the Fundamentals of Engineering (FE) exam, followed by the PE exam.
