Educational Requirements

Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA)

Duration: Typically 3-4 years

A BFA degree provides foundational skills in drawing, painting, sculpture, and multimedia art, as well as art history and theory.

Master of Fine Arts (MFA)

Duration: 2-3 years

An MFA offers advanced study in a specific area of artistic practice, allowing for specialization in painting, sculpture, or digital art.

Artistic Workshops and Residencies

Duration: Varies

Many artists further their skills and network by participating in workshops, residencies, and exhibitions at art institutions worldwide.
