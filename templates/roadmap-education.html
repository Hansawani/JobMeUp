Career Roadmap: Teaching Field

  • Complete High School The first step is to complete your 10+2 from a recognized board in India.
  • Pursue a Bachelor's Degree After completing high school, you need to pursue a bachelor's degree. Choose a subject that you are passionate about and would like to teach in the future.
  • Complete a B.Ed Degree Once you have completed your bachelor's degree, the next step is to pursue a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) degree. This is a mandatory qualification for teaching in high schools and higher classes in India.
  • Pass the Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) After completing your B.Ed, you need to pass the Teacher Eligibility Test (TET) conducted by both Central and State governments in India. This test certifies the eligibility of a candidate to teach in schools.
  • Gain Experience Try to gain some teaching experience. You can start as a trainee or an assistant teacher. This will give you practical exposure and help you understand the nuances of teaching.
  • Pursue a Master's Degree (Optional) If you wish to teach at the college level or higher, you will need to pursue a master's degree in your chosen subject. After your master's, you may also need to clear the National Eligibility Test (NET) to be eligible to teach at the university level.
  • Apply for Teaching Jobs Once you have the necessary qualifications and experience, you can start applying for teaching jobs. Keep an eye on the vacancies in different schools and colleges and apply accordingly.
