Career Roadmap: Engineering and Technology

  • Complete High School Focus on scoring well in your 10+2 exams.
  • Pursue a Bachelor's Degree in Engineering or Technology Choose a program in your preferred field.
  • Complete Bachelor's Degree Obtain your undergraduate degree in engineering or technology.
  • Gain Practical Experience Through Internships Seek internships to gain real-world experience.
  • Optional: Pursue a Master's Degree Consider advanced studies for career advancement.
  • Obtain Relevant Certifications Such as professional engineering licenses or specialized technology certifications.
  • Apply for Entry-Level Positions Tailor your application for roles in engineering and technology firms.
