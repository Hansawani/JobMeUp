Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Journalism or Communications

Duration: Typically 3-4 years

A Bachelor's degree in Journalism provides foundational skills in writing, reporting, media ethics, and multimedia production.

Master's Degree in Journalism (optional)

Duration: 1-2 years

For those looking to specialize or deepen their expertise, a Master's degree offers advanced training in investigative reporting, multimedia journalism, and global news coverage.

Internships and Field Experience

Duration: Varies

Most aspiring journalists gain hands-on experience through internships with newspapers, television stations, or online media platforms.
