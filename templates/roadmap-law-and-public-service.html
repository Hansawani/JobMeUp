Career Roadmap: Law and Public Service

  • High School (10+2) Focus on subjects like Political Science, History, and English which will help you develop a strong foundation for legal studies.
  • Entrance Exams Prepare for and take law entrance exams like CLAT (Common Law Admission Test), AILET (All India Law Entrance Test), etc. for admission into undergraduate law courses.
  • Undergraduate Studies Pursue a five-year integrated law course like BA LLB, BBA LLB, or BSc LLB from a recognized law college.
  • Internships Participate in internships during your law course to gain practical experience and understand the workings of the legal system.
  • Postgraduate Studies After your Bachelor's, you can opt for a Master's degree in Law (LLM) in your chosen specialty.
  • Legal Practice Start your legal practice in courts under the guidance of experienced lawyers. You may also need to register with the Bar Council of India to practice law in India.
  • Public Service After gaining some experience, you can prepare for competitive exams like the Civil Services Examination (CSE) conducted by UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) for a career in public service.
