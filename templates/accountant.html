Educational Requirements

Bachelor's Degree in Accounting or Finance

Duration: Typically 4 years

A Bachelor's degree in Accounting provides foundational knowledge in financial reporting, auditing, and tax laws.

Certified Public Accountant (CPA)

Duration: Varies (based on preparation for the CPA exam)

The CPA certification is essential for public accountants, demonstrating expertise in accounting and financial practices.

Master's Degree in Accounting

Duration: 1-2 years

A Master's in Accounting provides advanced knowledge in specialized areas such as forensic accounting, management accounting, and financial analysis.
